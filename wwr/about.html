History

Water Away White Water Rafting Company has been providing the best whitewater rafting experience in Colorado since 1974. As a family-owned and operated business, we take pride in offering exhilarating rafting trips on the Colorado and Roaring Fork Rivers in Glenwood Springs. Our location in Glenwood Springs allows us to explore the stunning Shoshone section of Glenwood Canyon and beyond. We offer a variety of trips, catering to families looking for a milder experience as well as to physically fit, experienced rafters seeking more challenging adventures. We are conveniently located an hour or less from Aspen, Snowmass, Eagle, Avon, Beaver Creek, Vail, and Grand Junction, making it easy for visitors from various areas to access our rafting experiences. Whether you're looking for a half-day adventure or a full-day excursion.
